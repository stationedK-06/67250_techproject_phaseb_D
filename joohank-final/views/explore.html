Mission

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the evolving relationship between art and time. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by showcasing historical and innovating arts.

Milestones

  • 2023: Expansion of the museum space and launch of international artist collaborations.
  • 2022: Launch of the Interactive Media Lab, allowing visitors to explore the exhibition online during coid-19
  • 2021: MonoMuse opens its doors with its first exhibition on classic art tour.
